# Product Overview

**Proxima Consulta** is a web application that automates appointment management and patient service via WhatsApp using Generative AI, designed for autonomous healthcare professionals (dentists, physiotherapists, etc.).

## Core Capabilities

1. **Appointment & Schedule Management** — Configure availability, sync with external calendars, and visualize daily/weekly agenda
2. **WhatsApp Automation with Generative AI** — Intelligent triage, self-service booking/rescheduling/cancellation, and automated reminders via natural language chat
3. **Patient Management (CRM)** — Automatic patient registration from WhatsApp interactions, appointment history, and smart waitlist
4. **Authentication & Access** — Social login (Google OAuth2), traditional email/password with 2FA, and account linking
5. **Billing & Subscription** — Integrated checkout with payment gateway, subscription plans with auto-renewal, billing dashboard, and 7-day free trial

## Target Use Cases

- Solo healthcare professionals who spend time and money on secretaries to manage their schedule and patients
- Professionals who want to reduce no-shows through automated confirmation reminders
- Practices that want to increase patient retention through efficient, always-available WhatsApp-based communication

## Value Proposition

Empowers autonomous health professionals to automate their entire appointment and patient management workflow through AI-powered WhatsApp interactions, reducing operational costs (no need for a secretary), increasing patient loyalty, and freeing up time to focus on clinical work.

## Business Model

SaaS subscription (Monthly, Semiannual, Annual) with a 7-day free trial requiring no credit card.
